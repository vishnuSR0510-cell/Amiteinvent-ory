Welcome to Amite Invent-ory

We are a group of passionate EEE students who have worked on various innovative projects in Electrical and Electronics Engineering. Our expertise lies in cutting-edge domains like Internet of Things (IoT), Electric Vehicles (EV), Smart Energy Systems, Embedded Systems, and more.

We are dedicated to solving real-world problems through our projects, ranging from home automation, energy monitoring, smart agriculture systems, and EV battery management to industrial automation and school-level science kits.

Whether you are a student, teacher, small business, or a budding startup, we are ready to take on mini-projects, school or college-level projects, as well as customized solutions for homes, shops, and small-scale industries.

We promise affordability, innovation, and a user-friendly approach in every solution we build!
